Let's Make Burrito

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 envelope taco seasoning
  • 1 can (16 ounces) refried beans
  • 6 flour tortillas (12 inches), warmed
  • 1 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese
  • 4 teaspoons canola oil
  • Sour cream and salsa

    Instructions:

  1. In a large cast-iron or other heavy skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.
  2. Stir in taco seasoning; remove from skillet and set aside. Wipe skillet clean.
  3. In a small saucepan, cook refried beans over medium-low heat until heated through, 2-3 minutes.
  4. Spoon about 1/3 cup of beans off-center on each tortilla; top with 1/4 cup beef mixture.
  5. Sprinkle with cheese. Fold sides and ends of tortillas over filling and roll up.
  6. In same skillet over medium-high heat, brown burritos in oil on all sides. Serve with sour cream and salsa.
